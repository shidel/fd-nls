Networking FreeDOS - 3Com

  If your 3Com NIC is not supported by the Crynwr packet drivers, you
  can find a driver here:
  ftp://ftp.3com.com/pub/nic/3c90x/3c90x2.exe (*01)
  This is EtherCD v5.4, disk 2 of 2 for the 3C90x adapter family. Disk 2
  contains DOS ODI and NDIS drivers, packet driver, help files, and DOS
  diagnostics.  This is a self-extracting DOS executable. After executing
  it, go into the PKTDVR subdirectory, and run 3C90XPD.COM. This should
  auto-detect your NIC, which may take a while, and install the packet
  driver in the first available interrupt vector, starting with 0x60.
  The interrupt vector can also specified with /I; see /? for details.
  You will want to add this to your AUTOEXEC.BAT.
  Afterwards, you can configure and use any WatTCP application, such as
  SSH (see: SSH2DOS) and Arachne. Use this in your WATTCP.CFG to
  specify the interrupt vector:
    pkt.vector = 0x60

  (*01) internet: "3c90x2.exe"
